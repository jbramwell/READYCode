# READYCode Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 30, 2026

## Overview

READYCode ("the App") is a BASIC code editor for the Commodore 64. This policy describes how Moonspace Labs, LLC ("we", "us") handles information in connection with your use of the App.

## Data We Collect

READYCode does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to Moonspace Labs or any third party. All data created or edited within the App remains local to your device.

The App communicates directly with devices you specify (Commodore 64 Ultimate or VICE emulator) over your local network solely to transfer code at your direction. No data from these transfers is retained or transmitted elsewhere.

## Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

- Personal identification information
- Usage or diagnostic data
- Location data
- Payment information
- Any data from children or adults

## Third-Party Services

READYCode does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

## Children's Privacy

READYCode does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date above reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the App following any changes.
